HTML Colors

HTML colors are specified with predefined color names, or with RGB, HEX, HSL, RGBA, or HSLA values.

Color values

rgb, rgba, hsl, hsla, Hex

Alpha means Transparent (alpha for div tag)
rgb means (Red, green, blue)
rgba means (Red, green, blue, alpha)
hsl means (Hue, saturation, Lightning)
hsla means (Hue, saturation, Lightning, alpha)
Hex means (hexadecimal) ex: #rrggbb
