Title: Love Beyond of the Wrong Time

In the vast and mysterious tapestry of existence, there existed a love so
extraordinary, so improbable, that it transcended the boundaries of time and
dimensions. Ryuo, a young man from a world untouched by the complexities of
interdimensional travel, found himself inexplicably drawn into a swirling
vortex that carried him to a realm unlike any he had ever known.
